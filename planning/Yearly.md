# 2025 Yearly Plan

## Overview
Theme: **Sustainable Growth with Newborn Life**
Focus: Tiny habits, low pressure, compounding results

---

## Q1: Newborn Survival Mode (Jan-Mar)

### Focus
- Adjust to new routine
- Support partner & baby
- Maintain minimal habits

### Tiny Habits (5-15 mins/day)
- [ ] **Morning:** Read 2 pages of technical article/book
- [ ] **Evening:** Journal one line about the day
- [ ] **Anytime:** Capture one business/product idea in notes

### Goals
- [ ] Survive first 3 months of parenthood
- [ ] Establish basic routine
- [ ] Build note repository of ideas (no action needed)

---

## Q2: Emerging Routine (Apr-Jun)

### Focus
- Baby sleep patterns stabilize
- Evaluate energy levels
- Light exploration

### Tiny Habits
- [ ] **3x/week:** 30 min learning session (AI/LLM/tech trends)
- [ ] **Daily:** Review & organize idea notes
- [ ] **Weekly:** One small experiment (API test, prototype sketch)

### Goals
- [ ] Complete one mini-course or tutorial series
- [ ] Validate 2-3 business ideas from notes
- [ ] Maintain health & family balance

---

## Q3: Low-Stakes Building (Jul-Sep)

### Focus
- Baby more independent
- 6+ months of sleep (hopefully!)
- Start small projects

### Tiny Habits
- [ ] **3x/week:** 1-hour project work (SaaS MVP, content, etc.)
- [ ] **Daily:** Write one tweet/thread about learning
- [ ] **Weekly:** Share progress publicly

### Goals
- [ ] Launch MVP of one small product/tool
- [ ] Build in public consistency
- [ ] Generate first side income (even $1 counts!)

---

## Q4: Momentum & Reflection (Oct-Dec)

### Focus
- Baby's first birthday approaching
- Evaluate what worked
- Plan 2026

### Tiny Habits
- [ ] **4x/week:** Dedicated project time (scale what worked)
- [ ] **Daily:** Document lessons learned
- [ ] **Monthly:** Review & adjust strategy

### Goals
- [ ] Stabilize or grow income stream
- [ ] Decide: double down or pivot for 2026
- [ ] Celebrate progress (not perfection!)

---

## Principles

1. **Family first** — no goal is worth missing milestones
2. **Progress, not perfection** — done is better than perfect
3. **Energy-aware** — skip habits on hard days, no guilt
4. **Compounding** — small daily actions > sporadic big efforts
5. **Flexible** — adjust quarterly based on reality

---

## Notes

- This plan assumes baby born ~March 2025
- Adjust timeline based on actual experience
- The goal is **maintenance and exploration**, not achievement
- 2026 is when serious building begins (if desired)
