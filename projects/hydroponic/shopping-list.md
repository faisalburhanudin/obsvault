# Hydroponic Media Bed - Shopping List

Complete list of items needed to build the Media Bed system from *DIY Hydroponic Gardens* by Tyler Baras.

---

## 📦 Containers

| Item | Qty | Specs | Estimated Price (IDR) | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|----------------------|-------|
| **Grow Bed** | 1 | ~36 cm L x ~28 cm W x **~8-10 cm H** plastic container | 15,000 - 50,000 | Shallow tray/tote. Bottom should fit inside reservoir. |
| **Reservoir** | 1 | ~37 cm L x ~27 cm W x **~20-25 cm H** plastic container (~15-20 L) | 25,000 - 60,000 | Deeper than grow bed. Must hold water volume + pump. |

**Shopping tip:** Look for "tempat makanan" or storage containers at Mitra10, Ace Hardware, or supermarkets. The grow bed should sit on top with its lip overhanging.

| Item | Qty | Specs | Estimated Price (IDR) | Where to Find |
|------|-----|-------|----------------------|---------------|

**Pump alternatives:**
- Atman AT-301/AT-302 (~600-800 L/h)
- Resun SP-980 (~800 L/h)
- Yamano WP-300 (~600 L/h)

## 🛒 Where to Buy in Indonesia

### Online (Recommended for price comparison)
- **Tokopedia/Shopee:** Search "alat hidroponik", "bulkhead fitting", "pompa celup", "hydroton"
- Filter by location for faster shipping

### Physical Stores
- **Hydroponic specialty shops:** Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya, Yogyakarta (Google "toko hidroponik")
- **Ace Hardware / Mitra10:** Containers, tools, spray paint
- **Aquarium shops:** Pumps, tubing
- **Building/hardware stores (Bangunan):** Drills, drill bits, tools

### Budget Alternatives
- **Grow Bed:** Shallow plastic food containers (kotak makan) ~10,000-20,000
- **Reservoir:** Large water jugs or storage boxes ~20,000-40,000
- **Fittings:** Sometimes can DIY with grommets + PVC fittings (more advanced)

## 🎯 Starter Kit Alternative

Many hydroponic shops sell starter kits that include:
- Pump
- Fittings
- Tubing
- Timer
- Sometimes nutrients

**Price:** ~200,000 - 350,000

Then you just need:
- Containers (50,000 - 100,000)
- Clay pebbles (80,000 - 150,000)
- Tools (if you don't have) (50,000 - 100,000)
