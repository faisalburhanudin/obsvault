TOMATOES Most tomato varieties can be grown in hydroponics, I encourage you to try any varieties, especially if you already have a favorite tomato variety. The varieties below are some of my favorite tomato varieties I've tried in hydroponics. Tomatoes are generally grown in full sun but many can grow well in partial shade. Most seed packets will instruct gardeners to grow tomatoes at 60 to 75°F, if possible. Many of these varieties do great at higher temperatures, especially cherry tomatoes. Cherry tomatoes can tolerate temperatures over 90°F.
